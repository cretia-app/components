# Welcome to Components by Cretia

A collection of reusable and customizable user interface elements designed with a foundational base design.

These components are crafted to simplify and enhance the development of your applications.

With Cretia Components, you can effortlessly integrate pre-built UI elements into your projects while maintaining control
over their appearance and behavior, all while benefiting from a cohesive and well-thought-out design system.

## Design Tokens

Predefined values for essential design properties like colors, typography, spacing, and more.

These tokens are accessible as CSS custom properties via the @cretia/components stylesheet.

By adhering to these design tokens, you can ensure a cohesive and polished look and feel for your applications.

## Components

Our components are designed to be flexible, responsive, and customizable to fit seamlessly into your projects.

Whether you're building a web application, mobile app, or anything in between, Cretia Components has you covered.
